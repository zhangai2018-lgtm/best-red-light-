## The 10Hz Pulse Mode: What It Actually Does

Most panels at this price just give you on/off for each wavelength. The BioHeal Plus adds an optional 10Hz pulse mode, and it deserves a closer look because the marketing copy on this feature ranges from accurate to overstated.

Pulsed light in red light therapy is based on research suggesting that certain frequencies of pulsed delivery may produce different cellular responses than continuous wave delivery. 10Hz is a specific frequency that has appeared in some neurological research contexts (though the evidence base for specific pulsed frequencies is still developing).

Practically, what this means for a home user: you have an additional mode to experiment with. Some people report finding pulsed sessions feel different from continuous — though whether that's a genuine therapeutic difference or perception is hard to say at the individual level.

What it's definitely not: magic. The core of what this panel does is deliver 660nm and 850nm photons to your tissue. The pulse mode is an additional feature, not the reason to buy or skip it.
