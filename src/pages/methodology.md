At RedPanelLab, our rankings are based on an editorial review process.

When comparing red light therapy panels, we consider:

- Panel size and full-body coverage
- Wavelength configuration, such as 660nm and 850nm
- LED count and treatment area
- Mounting options and ease of setup
- Manufacturer specifications
- Warranty and return terms
- Brand reputation and support
- Overall value for the intended use

Our editorial ratings are internal scores created by RedPanelLab. They are not Amazon customer reviews, Amazon star ratings, or ratings provided by manufacturers.

We do not accept paid rankings. Brands cannot pay to receive a higher position on our lists.

Product details, pricing, availability, warranty terms, and return policies can change over time. We encourage readers to check the current Amazon listing before purchasing.
